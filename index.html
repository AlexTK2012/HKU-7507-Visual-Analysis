Visual Analysis of influential films’ composition

Description

This project will provide real-time monitoring and analysing of the public’s attention and satisfaction on air quality. We will develop filters to obtain air quality related messages, based on keyword matching, from the most popular Chinese microblog platforms Sina Weibo. We put the messages into a model, using Spark Streaming to implement real-time analysing and derive results on how dissatisfied the public is about the air quality in their city. The results will be demonstrated in chart format for friendly view. In addition, the results may promote government information disclosure and provide evidence and recommendations for government decision-making.
