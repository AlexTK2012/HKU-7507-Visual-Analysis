    Description

    When people define a successful movie, revenue and average rate are two most frequently mentioned factors. For the audience, they want to know if the unreleased movie is worth watching. More importantly, from a business perspective, companies desire to understand how to make great works. In this research, we analyze The Movie Database which has almost 5000 movies, including attributes such as name, genre, actor, director, average rate, revenue, budget, runtime, original language. We want to seek for the definition for influential movies in this process , solve problems how genre and budget, effect on rate and revenue, get the answer for good actors and directors, and find reliable production company. Finally, to find out which elements are the main affecting factors by considering these factors.

    Overview

    Overview represents about 5000 films’ revenue and average rate. Sizes describe how large the box offices are and colors show rating of movies. When the mouse hovering over one specific data, the values of revenue and rate will be given. In those films with both high rate and large box office, we can see films like The Lord of the Rings, Inception, The Lion King, Avatar.

    Composition Analysis

    From the box-and-whisker plots of genre and vote average, we can find that the popular genre films like comedy and action have a wider range of rate, and a lower average rate. However, the niche films like western have more narrow range of rate and higher average rate. It is because the popular genre films usually recieve more rates, so the average rate trend to be a middle level, but for the niche films, only the core fans will vote for them and they usually trend to give higher rates.

    There are some rules we can find from the box-and-whisker plots of genre and revenue. It is seen that the popular genres like adventure, animation, and family usually have higher revenue. We think it is because these popular genre movies have more audiences. However, the niche genres like documentary usually have lower revenue.

    We explore if runtime have influence on films’ rate and revenue or not. We use opposite color, red and blue to represent different runtimes. From our analysis, there are some relations between runtime and revenue. We believe the movie with runtime longer than 2 hours will perform better.

    We use all movies records, almost 5000 records, to observe the law between release time and revenue. We can see that from May to July, the number of released movies are less while the box office is huge. Oppositely, the film industry has intense competition and not enough revenue from August to October. Thus, we recommend May, June, July, November and December to release the films.

    We want to explore the law between budget and the influential of movies. Using color to distinguish films’ rates. Clicking the line in the graph, we could see the formula between revenue and budget which could show there is a clear and strong positive correlation. And also, we can see that movies with high rates concentrated above the line, that’s to say, the box office of high rating movies is at least three times the budget in most instance.

    Network

    Actors who have appeared in these Top 100 movies as main actors (Actor 1 or 2) are selected as nodes and so are directors. Each edge in this network shows that the two persons have collaborated in the same movie. This network is composed of 209 persons (nodes) and 295 edges. The color of nodes is using to distinguish Actor with Director. Hovering over the nodes in this network will show the character name, cooperated people and cooperation times. Hovering over the edges in this network will show the cooperation times between the two persons. We can zoom in to see a sub-network’s details clearly.
